## Fabrication stop

Do not order PCBs from this baseline. Zero DRC errors only proves that the current placed geometry has no error-severity violation. It does not close the 117 common-board or 21-per-adapter open connections.

Physical evidence is still missing for:

- XIAO board revision and bottom-pad mapping.
- USB and BLE enumeration/reconnection.
- RGB rail switching, maximum current, reverse current, and battery charging.
- Touch sensitivity under enclosure, RGB, USB, and charging noise.
- RF keep-out and battery/antenna interaction.
- Connector mating, USB cable strain, stabilizer fit, switch/socket retention, and repeated actuation.
